Statistical Analysis & OLS Regression

Video Game Global Market Strategy

Using regression modeling and hypothesis testing, this project mapped market behavior across genres, regions, and release eras to inform strategy.

OLS Modeled sales patterns and regional differences.
ANOVA Validated genre and platform performance differences.
Chi-Square Tested relationships between categorical consumer variables.

Approach

  • Cleaned and standardized longitudinal market data.
  • Applied OLS regression to estimate influential factors.
  • Used ANOVA and chi-square methods to validate assumptions.

Outcomes

  • Improved confidence in regional targeting decisions.
  • Highlighted which genres were most resilient across markets.
  • Created a repeatable framework for market insight.
