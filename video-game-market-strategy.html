Video Game Global Market Strategy

This case study explores how gameplay genre, region, and historical release patterns shape sales performance in a global interactive entertainment market.

Global Lens Mapped regional demand across multiple game markets.
Genre Signals Identified which game styles were most consistent performers.
Strategy Ready Translated statistical findings into clearer market targeting.

How the analysis worked

  • Built a historical dataset covering sales, genre, region, and release period.
  • Applied OLS regression to isolate the strongest market drivers.
  • Used ANOVA and chi-square testing to validate genre and audience patterns.

What it revealed

  • Showed where market demand was strongest and where it was weakening.
  • Highlighted the genres most likely to resonate across regions.
  • Provided a more defensible framework for market positioning.
