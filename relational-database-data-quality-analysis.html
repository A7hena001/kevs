Relational Database Management & Data Quality Analysis

This project centered on building a reliable relational structure and measuring how missing data could interfere with analysis.

Relational Design Structured data for robust querying and relationship analysis.
Missing Data Audit Quantified data gaps and their effect on interpretation.
Quality Reporting Documented the risk of drawing conclusions from incomplete fields.

Work

  • Designed tables and relationships for analytical use.
  • Audited missing data across core dimensions.
  • Highlighted where integrity issues could distort outcomes.

Outcome

  • Improved confidence in data-driven conclusions.
  • Created a stronger baseline for later analysis.
  • Made data quality visible and actionable.
