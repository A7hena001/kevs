Behavioral Analytics & Clinical Insights

This project translated complex biomedical cohort data into a clear visual and analytical language for research teams and clinical stakeholders.

Patient Flow Mapped how groups and biomarkers moved through the dataset.
Clinical Context Connected patterns to real research questions and decision points.
Cross-Team Insight Made complex findings easier to share across disciplines.

What the work looked like

  • Built hierarchical visuals such as sunburst and alluvial diagrams.
  • Structured biomedical data into interpretable cohort views.
  • Focused on making hidden relationships visible to non-technical audiences.

Why it mattered

  • Helped stakeholders interpret nuanced clinical patterns faster.
  • Reduced friction between data exploration and decision-making.
  • Turned a technical dataset into a shared research asset.
