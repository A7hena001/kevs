YOLOv5 vs. YOLOv8 Performance Evaluation

This project compared two YOLO model generations on aerial images, measuring accuracy and generalization under constrained data.

mAP Used for model quality and detection confidence comparisons.

Evaluation

  • Benchmarked precision and recall across models.
  • Assessed detection performance on aerial imagery.
  • Compared anchor-based and anchor-free approaches.

Takeaway

  • YOLOv5 generalized better in the tested conditions.
  • Trade-offs were documented for future model selection.
  • Results informed a practical deployment decision-making framework.
