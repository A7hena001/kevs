YOLOv5 vs. YOLOv8 Performance Evaluation

This project compared two leading object detection models in a visually demanding aerial setting, where image scale, density, and small-object recognition matter most.

18 Variants Measured across a wide range of training and tuning settings.
Detection Metrics Evaluated precision, recall, and mAP for each model.
Real-World Fit Focused on which model generalized better under limited data.

How the evaluation was framed

  • Tested detection performance on aerial imagery rather than standard benchmark sets.
  • Compared anchor-based and anchor-free model behavior.
  • Looked beyond raw scores to understand practical trade-offs.

What the results suggest

  • YOLOv5 showed stronger generalization in the tested setting.
  • Model choice depends on the balance between accuracy and efficiency.
  • The work informs how to select vision systems for constrained deployments.
