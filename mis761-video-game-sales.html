MIS761 Video Game Sales Analysis

This report used statistical modeling and hypothesis testing to understand how regional markets, genres, and brands shape global video game sales.

Regional Trends

Demonstrated Skills

  • ANOVA, t-tests, chi-square testing, and regression
  • Regional market analysis and consumer behavior interpretation
  • Communication of insights for product and release strategy

Project Summary

  • Examined sales patterns across North America, Europe, Japan, and other regions over multiple decades.
  • Identified strong genre and brand differences by geography and confirmed that these differences were statistically significant.
  • Used regression to evaluate which factors had the strongest association with market share.

Overall Project Summary

The project offers a practical framework for market strategy, showing that consumer preferences vary sharply by region and that data-driven release planning should be tailored rather than generalized.
