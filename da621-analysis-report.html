DA621 Analysis Report

This report explored how budgets, audience ratings, and character counts relate to the commercial success of the Harry Potter films.

Demonstrated Skills

  • Exploratory data analysis and data merging
  • Correlation analysis and hypothesis testing
  • Clear statistical storytelling and interpretation

Project Summary

  • Combined multiple datasets to build a structured analysis of box office, budget, audience rating, and character count.
  • Found a strong negative relationship between revenue and IMDb rating and a moderate positive relationship between budget and revenue.
  • Used p-values and correlations to test whether the observed relationships were statistically meaningful.

Overall Project Summary

The project shows that financial performance is not just a byproduct of story content. Instead, it reflects a combination of financial investment, audience reception, and market dynamics that should be assessed with care and rigor.
