MIS776 Executive Summary

Data Engineering and Market Analysis

This project turns e-commerce transaction data into a refined decision-support workflow through data cleaning, market-basket analysis, pricing insights, and text clustering.

Demonstrated Skills

  • Data cleaning and preparation for transactional retail datasets
  • Association-rule mining and market-basket analysis
  • TF-IDF feature engineering and unsupervised clustering

Project Summary

  • Filtered missing and negative entries to create a reliable transactional dataset.
  • Used Apriori rules to uncover product affinities and evaluate how order size affects co-purchase behavior.
  • Clustered product descriptions to identify meaningful inventory themes and support smarter merchandising.

Overall Project Summary

The project shows how data engineering and analytics can come together to produce business value. By combining structured transaction analysis with text-based inventory segmentation, the work offers practical guidance for promotions, restocking, and customer-focused strategy.
