Cinematic Success & Market Trend Analysis

This project examined how budget, genre, and audience response connect to commercial outcomes in film markets.

6.4K+ Rows of film and revenue data analyzed.
Correlation Uncovered non-obvious relationships between ratings and revenue.
Outlier Control Removed distortions to build a cleaner model space.

Method

  • Standardized and cleaned the dataset.
  • Filtered extreme cases that would skew trends.
  • Used correlation analysis to surface meaningful patterns.

Value

  • Helped interpret audience reception more clearly.
  • Improved decision-making around production and positioning.
  • Created an analytical narrative around market signals.
