DA621 Analysis Report

Statistical Analysis and Visualization

This project explores how film-level attributes such as budget, audience rating, and character count relate to revenue in the Harry Potter franchise.

Demonstrated Skills

  • Exploratory data analysis and data preparation
  • Correlation analysis and hypothesis testing
  • Communication of statistical findings through narrative reporting

Project Summary

  • Combined multiple datasets and standardized features for a consistent analysis.
  • Detected a strong negative relationship between revenue and audience rating and a moderate positive relationship between budget and revenue.
  • Found that the number of unique characters had only a weak relationship with commercial success.

Overall Project Summary

The analysis shows that franchise performance is driven by a mix of financial investment and audience reception rather than just narrative structure. The work demonstrates how careful statistical reporting can uncover non-intuitive insights and guide future investigation.
